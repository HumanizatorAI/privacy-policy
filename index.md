## Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data

Humanizator **does not require account registration** and **does not intentionally collect personal data** such as name, address, phone number, or payment information.

Any text entered by the user is used **only for text humanization purposes** and is **not stored permanently** by the Application.

#### Usage Data

Usage Data may be collected automatically and may include:

- Device type and operating system
- Application version
- Anonymous diagnostic or crash data

This data is used solely to ensure application stability and improve performance.

---

## Use of Your Data

The Company may use data only for the following purposes:

- To provide and maintain the Service
- To process text entered by the user for humanization
- To improve application performance and user experience
- To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

## Third-Party Services

The Application may use third-party services (such as AI text processing providers) **strictly for performing text humanization**.
